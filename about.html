How RateMyCourse was created

June 29, 2021

RateMyCourse was created to allow students from different universites to check with others in their university to see which courses would be suitable for them based on their majors and class needed left before graduation. This website allows people to check with other students in their major to see what classes they can take and whether or not it would this class would be suitable for them. You can filter through enjoyment of the class, diffuctly, overall experience, and grade recieved.

How Was The Idea Created

I first discovered this idea, as there were many people I saw struggling find courses in time before registration was being filled up. This gave me the idea to create a platform for people to discover courses for that fulfill their major requirements and would be a enjoyable class for them, as a lot of times this not been the case for me.

What Has Been Done So Far

Currently, the website allows for people to enter their university, and see the enjoyment of the class, diffuctly,overall experience, and grade recieved. Students are able to give a one through five star rating out of these three filters. You can also see what professors students have taken and see if this professor has gotten good reviews for that class.

What Will Need To Be Done(Updates)

We will be implementing more filters for people. We will also in the future allow for suggestions to people based on the classes they have taken and see if that class would be a good match for them. This will allow students to see what classes are suited for them, and tailor them to pick their classes based on the indicators
